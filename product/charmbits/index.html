Product Design

CharmBits

Summary

CharmBits are modular circuit wearables that teach children the basics of electronics. This project was created during UC Berkeley's course Interactive Device Design in Fall 2014. Throughout the course of a semester, student teams work together to design an interactive device combining the fields of UI design, 3D modeling, and electronics.
