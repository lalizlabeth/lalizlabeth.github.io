How do you become a good designer?

Many of you have been asking me this question through email! There isn't a formula. I wish I could give you a step-by-step guide, but the truth is that it differs per person. Here are a few things to consider on your journey:

  • Understand typography: No matter what field in design you want to pursue, having a strong typographic sense is extremely important. To get started, learn how to use one typeface effectively to create hierarchy.
  • Define your personal style: Develop your own aesthetic! In the design industry, especially with graphic design, it's easy to get caught up in trends on Dribbble. Remember that everything on Dribbble starts to look the same after you've scrolled through a few pages. Having an interesting perspective will help you stand out.
  • Be able to explain everything: You should be able to give a reason for every part of a design in your portfolio. Why is this button green? Why is this button in the top-right corner?
  • Read read read: Read blogs and books. Really anything about design! This will help you learn how to defend your designs and define your personal style.
