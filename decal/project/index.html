Projects

All assignments are submitted through Behance. These will be graded based on completion and effort. The final project will be to create a portfolio showcasing all your work.

Assignment 1: Repetition- Due: Tuesday September 16 at 11:59pm

Design a postcard(s) for your favorite fictional city or world. The postcard(s) must express the theme of repetition. You can create as many postcards as you wish as long as they all connect with each other. Your postcards should be 4in by 6in.

Upload your assignment to your Behance and add @innod-decal as a collaborator. Put a brief explanation for each postcard.

Assignment 2: Wash yo hands- Due: Tuesday September 22 at 11:59pm

Design an advertisement to encourage students between the ages of K-12 to wash their hands in the bathroom. The important part of this assignment is to show your design process!

Upload your assignment to your Behance and add @innod-decal (Innovative Design DeCal) as a collaborator. Don't forget to upload steps to your design process!

Assignment 3: Trio of Emotions- Due: Tuesday September 29 at 11:59pm

Choose an inanimate object (chair, cupcake, telephone, etc.) and illustrate it. Make three copies of that object and color each a different way so that each panel represents a different emotion (happiness, anger, confusion, etc.). Do not edit the paths or add additional shapes. Do not put the emotion explicitly within the image because weâ€™ll try to guess each emotion during critique next week. Write a brief description of why and how you chose each color scheme.

Upload your assignment to your Behance and add @innod-decal (Innovative Design DeCal) as a collaborator.

Assignment 5: Type Experimentation- Due: Wednesday October 15 at 11:59pm

Option 1: Create a Drop Cap. Illustrate it and digitize it with Illustrator or Photoshop. This will be worth two homework assignments!

Option 2: Create a typography poster of your favorite quote.

Upload your assignment to your Behance and add @innod-decal (Innovative Design DeCal) as a collaborator.

Assignment 7: App Redesign- Due: Wednesday October 28 at 11:59pm

Choose an app of your choice. Redesign 1 or more screens. Try to choose an app that you believe has many usability isses.

Upload your assignment to your Behance and add @innod-decal (Innovative Design DeCal) as a collaborator.
