About the Design Minor

Cal is introducing a design minor in Fall 2014 as a supplement to the Jacobs Institute of Design which will be opening in Fall 2015. Throughout the year, I've worked with a team of students to help shape the future of the minor and the institute. We researched existing student organizations, new teaching methodologies, and current design courses.
