About BARE

BARE is Cal's fashion and arts publication. The magazine is published semesterly and completely student-run. As the Layout Director from 2013-2014, I led and directed the style of issue 14 and 15. I created style guidelines for both issues by setting type and grid guidelines.

For issue 15, BARE received CSPA (Columbia Scholastic Press Association) awards:

  • 1st place, "Speciality Magazine Overall Design"
  • Certificate of Mention, "Cover Design"
  • Certificate of Mention, "Table of Contents Design"
