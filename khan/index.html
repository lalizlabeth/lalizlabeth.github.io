This past summer, I was fortunate enough to be a Product Design intern on Khan Academy's iOS team. Over the course of three months, my team launched math exercises onto the iPad app. Because Khan gives interns a great deal of autonomy, I had the opportunity to lead design projects outside the iOS team. These projects ranged from revamping the interns recruitment page to creating invites for intern tea time. Below, you'll find details about my internship.
